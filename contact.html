Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping & delivery timeline?
We ship worldwide. Standard Shipping takes 3-5 business days, while Express Shipping takes 1-2 business days. Shipping is completely free on all orders valued above ₹200.
What is your product return policy?
We support a 30-day hassle-free return window. If you are unsatisfied with your premium product, you can request a return directly from your Order Dashboard. We cover return shipping label costs.
How can I track my active order status?
Once your payment is verified, you can track delivery in real time on the Order Tracking tab using the unique ID sent to your email or by going to My Orders inside your Profile Dashboard.
Are the electronics covered under warranty?
Yes, all premium audio, laptop, smartphone, and home appliances are covered under a 1-Year Manufacturer Warranty. Warranty claims can be submitted via email to our support center.
